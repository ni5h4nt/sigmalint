# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) verbatim.

In short: be respectful, focus on the work, assume good intent, and engage in good faith. Harassment, personal attacks, and bad-faith argumentation are not welcome.

## Reporting

Report incidents to the project maintainer at `nishant.tyagi@gmail.com` with the subject line `sigmalint conduct`. Reports are confidential.

The full text of the Contributor Covenant v2.1, including the enforcement guidelines, is available at the link above and is incorporated here by reference.
